<p style='margin-top:0cm;margin-right:0cm;margin-bottom:0cm;margin-left:17.85pt;line-height:normal;font-size:16px;font-family:"Aptos",sans-serif;text-indent:-17.85pt;'><strong>Stop Guessing! Start Tracking!</strong><br>&nbsp;<br>&nbsp;Unlock your team&apos;s full potential with the ultimate volleyball stat tracker. Our app provides data-driven insights to make informed decisions and develop your athletes&apos; skills. Track live stats during matches and practices, capturing detailed performance metrics with just a tap. Analyze player development, identify growth opportunities, and create targeted practice plans based on real data.</p>
<p style='margin-top:0cm;margin-right:0cm;margin-bottom:0cm;margin-left:17.85pt;line-height:normal;font-size:16px;font-family:"Aptos",sans-serif;text-indent:-17.85pt;'><strong>Key Features:<br>&nbsp;</strong></p>
<p style='margin-top:0cm;margin-right:0cm;margin-bottom:0cm;margin-left:17.85pt;line-height:normal;font-size:16px;font-family:"Aptos",sans-serif;'><strong><span style="color:#215F9A;">Comprehensive Data Collection</span></strong></p>
<ul style="list-style-type: disc;margin-left: 25.85px;">
    <li>Live Stat Taking: Record match and practice stats in real time. Track first ball wins/losses, rally outcomes, service aces/errors, kills, blocks, and various errors.</li>
    <li>Performance Metrics: Monitor scoring streaks and side out/breakpoint statistics for players and team.</li>
    <li>Roster Management: Add/edit/remove players from your teams and opponent teams.<br>&nbsp;</li>
</ul>
<p style='margin-top:0cm;margin-right:0cm;margin-bottom:0cm;margin-left:-.15pt;line-height:normal;font-size:16px;font-family:"Aptos",sans-serif;text-indent:18.0pt;'><strong><span style="color:#215F9A;">Versatile Application Suite</span></strong></p>
<ul style="list-style-type: disc;margin-left: 25.85px;">
    <li>Multiple Apps in One Account: Three integrated apps&mdash;side out/breakpoint, gamebreaker, and serve-receive&mdash;usable together on one device or across two.</li>
    <li>User-Friendly Interface: Quick stat entry on tablet or phone, by one or two stat takers, capturing every meaningful play.<br>&nbsp;</li>
</ul>
<p style='margin-top:0cm;margin-right:0cm;margin-bottom:0cm;margin-left:17.85pt;line-height:normal;font-size:16px;font-family:"Aptos",sans-serif;'><strong><span style="color:#215F9A;">Data-Driven Development Tools</span></strong></p>
<ul style="list-style-type: disc;margin-left: 26.15px;">
    <li>&nbsp;Session &amp; Game Summaries: Review performance with detailed reports highlighting strengths and development areas.</li>
    <li>Comprehensive Season Data: Track progress with cumulative summaries. Filter by date, team, location, or match type.</li>
    <li>Easy Data Export: Share insights through comprehensive PDFs.<br>&nbsp;</li>
</ul>
<p style='margin-top:0cm;margin-right:0cm;margin-bottom:0cm;margin-left:18.15pt;line-height:normal;font-size:16px;font-family:"Aptos",sans-serif;'><strong><span style="color:#215F9A;">Educational Benefits</span></strong></p>
<ul style="list-style-type: disc;margin-left: 26.15px;">
    <li>Targeted Practice Planning: Identify skill areas needing attention for focused sessions.</li>
    <li>Player Development Tracking: Help athletes visualize progress and set goals.</li>
    <li>Team Dynamics Analysis: Optimize rotations based on player combinations.</li>
    <li>Evidence-Based Coaching: Replace subjective impressions with concrete data.<br>&nbsp;</li>
</ul>
<p style='margin-top:0cm;margin-right:0cm;margin-bottom:0cm;margin-left:0cm;line-height:normal;font-size:16px;font-family:"Aptos",sans-serif;'><strong>Three Powerful Apps:</strong></p>
<p style='margin-top:0cm;margin-right:0cm;margin-bottom:0cm;margin-left:0cm;line-height:normal;font-size:16px;font-family:"Aptos",sans-serif;'><strong><span style="color:#215F9A;">Game Breaker - Track Point Production and Game Flow</span></strong></p>
<p style='margin-top:0cm;margin-right:0cm;margin-bottom:0cm;margin-left:0cm;line-height:normal;font-size:16px;font-family:"Aptos",sans-serif;'><br>&nbsp;Reveals who&apos;s contributing to success and where improvements are needed:</p>
<ul style="list-style-type: disc;">
    <li>Identify point scorers and point givers</li>
    <li>Track scoring runs for informed timeout decisions</li>
    <li>Analyze impactful servers on both teams</li>
    <li>Pinpoint strongest/weakest rotations</li>
    <li>Motivate athletes with transparent performance data</li>
    <li>Make data-driven substitutions</li>
</ul>
<p style='margin-top:0cm;margin-right:0cm;margin-bottom:0cm;margin-left:36.0pt;line-height:normal;font-size:16px;font-family:"Aptos",sans-serif;'>&nbsp;</p>
<p style='margin-top:0cm;margin-right:0cm;margin-bottom:0cm;margin-left:36.0pt;line-height:normal;font-size:16px;font-family:"Aptos",sans-serif;margin:0cm;'><strong><span style="color:#215F9A;">Sideout/BreakPoint - Master the Serve and First Ball Battle</span></strong></p>
<p style='margin-top:0cm;margin-right:0cm;margin-bottom:0cm;margin-left:36.0pt;line-height:normal;font-size:16px;font-family:"Aptos",sans-serif;margin:0cm;'><br>&nbsp;Digs deeper into the crucial first phase of every rally:</p>
<p style='margin-top:0cm;margin-right:0cm;margin-bottom:0cm;margin-left:36.0pt;line-height:normal;font-size:16px;font-family:"Aptos",sans-serif;margin:0cm;'>&nbsp;</p>
<ul style="list-style-type: disc;">
    <li>Discover which servers create the most breakpoint opportunities</li>
    <li>Identify troublesome opponent servers</li>
    <li>Analyze rotation-specific sideout performance</li>
    <li>Access comprehensive first-ball data</li>
    <li>Focus practice on specific rotations</li>
    <li>Develop strategic serves for breakpoint opportunities</li>
    <li>Track sideout percentage improvements<br>&nbsp;</li>
</ul>
<p style='margin-top:0cm;margin-right:0cm;margin-bottom:0cm;margin-left:36.0pt;line-height:normal;font-size:16px;font-family:"Aptos",sans-serif;margin:0cm;'><strong><span style="color:#215F9A;">Serve vs Serve Receive - Elevate Practice Intensity with Purpose</span></strong></p>
<p style='margin-top:0cm;margin-right:0cm;margin-bottom:0cm;margin-left:36.0pt;line-height:normal;font-size:16px;font-family:"Aptos",sans-serif;margin:0cm;'><br>&nbsp;Transform routine drills into competitive challenges:</p>
<p style='margin-top:0cm;margin-right:0cm;margin-bottom:0cm;margin-left:36.0pt;line-height:normal;font-size:16px;font-family:"Aptos",sans-serif;margin:0cm;'>&nbsp;</p>
<ul style="list-style-type: disc;">
    <li>Create competitions between servers and passers</li>
    <li>Raise stakes with data-driven challenges</li>
    <li>Determine objectively who the best passers are</li>
    <li>Identify problematic serve types</li>
    <li>Bring purpose to serve and pass drills</li>
    <li>Track improvement with detailed metrics</li>
    <li>Customize drills based on match weaknesses<br>&nbsp;</li>
</ul>
